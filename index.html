About me

I've always wanted to blend creativity, convenience, and improving quality of life into my work, aiming to create technology that makes life better and easier. I pursued biochemistry with the goal of becoming a pharmacist, believing I could enhance people’s lives by providing essential medications for their health and well-being. Since a career as a software engineer was out of reach at the time, I saw pharmacy as a way to help others, much like how software engineers bridge technology and users, delivering solutions that improve everyday life. However, while working in labs during my studies and later with pharmacy software as a technician, I noticed many systems lacked intuitive design and could be far more efficient.

As a full-stack software engineer with a foundation in healthcare and science, I bring a unique perspective on how technology can enhance the healthcare system. My experience in these fields has highlighted numerous opportunities to refine software and UI/UX design for greater efficiency and effectiveness. I have developed a keen understanding of the complexities within healthcare systems and am driven to leverage my skills in JavaScript, Python, and full-stack development to create innovative solutions that improve system functionality and user experience.

Beyond my professional work, I'm a houseplant enthusiast who enjoys taking care of my plants, I love cats, and like to create cat-themed projects. I’m also passionate about organization and enjoy applying my skills to personal projects.

I’m excited to combine my healthcare experience with my technical skills to develop software that meets users' needs and contributes to a tech-driven future where technology improves both daily life and various industries. Collaborating with others to create technologies that address diverse needs and drive meaningful progress is something I’m particularly enthusiastic about.
